# Product Definition: Chatinho

## Summary
Chatinho is a full-stack chatbot system integrated with WhatsApp (via Evolution API) and AI (OpenAI). It supports both automated interactions through predefined flows/AI and human handovers with service queues.

## Problem Statement
Companies need a centralized, automated, and scalable way to handle WhatsApp customer service without relying solely on expensive official API solutions or manual attendance.

## Target Users
- Small to Medium Enterprises (SMEs) needing customer support automation.
- Customer support teams (Attendants).
- System Administrators.

## Key Goals
- Real-time WhatsApp integration with QR Code connection.
- Automated service with intelligent AI-driven responses.
- Human service queue with seamless handovers.
- Administrative dashboard for monitoring and management.
